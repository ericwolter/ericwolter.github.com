Molicula

Description

Molicula is simple puzzle game. Rotate and flip the pieces to fill the grid. Only 6 out of the 7 pieces are required to complete the grid. Which piece you leave out determines the difficulty of finding a solution.

Can you find all solutions?

  • Simple graphics
  • Intuitive gestures
  • Over 40 unique solutions

Molicula runs on iPad 2 and higher.

Technologies

  • iOS SDK
  • ObjectiveC
  • OpenGL
